About The Barkeep

Welcome, weary traveler! Sean is a software engineer from Philly. He plays many different kinds of games, and explores the outdoors often. He hopes that technology can empower us to make the world better.


He tries to be a good friend, family member and citizen. He is an alumni of the University of Pennsylvania. A fellow of Venture for America. An eagle scout. A volunteer with Code for Philly. And a co-founder of PennApps Fellowship Program. Among other things...


This humble establishment serves as the heart of my digital domain - a central gathering place for all my projects and creations. Please browse and make yourself comfortable!
